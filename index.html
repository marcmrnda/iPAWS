Enhanced Pet Lifestyle

How Can We Help Elevate Your Pet's Lifestyle?

AI is at the heart of iPAWS, driving its intelligence and functionality. From interpreting vital signs in real-time to analyzing data for mood predictions, iPAWS harnesses the power of machine learning to understand and anticipate the unique needs of each pet. The Pet Profile Generator uses AI to analyze personality traits, behaviors, and needs, creating a comprehensive report. Our Recommendation System utilizes AI algorithms to suggest tailored products and services. iPAWS isn't just a smart collar; it's an AI-driven marvel that elevates the pet care experience.

Geofencing & Pet Tracking

Create virtual barriers to monitor your pet's location and receive alerts when they cross boundaries. GPS technology for accurate real-time location tracking, enhancing pet safety and monitoring.

Vital Sign Tracking

Real-time monitoring of your pet's vital signs, including heart rate, temperature, and activity levels, combined with GPS tracking to detect early health issues, prevent accidents, and provide instant alerts and location updates for enhanced safety and well-being.

Mood Prediction

Utilizing vital signs and motion sensors, iPAWS analyzes pet behavior to predict their mood accurately through data integration and machine learning.

Sleep & Activity Surveillance

iPAWS monitors pet sleep duration and activity patterns, providing insights into their well-being and helping owners ensure they get sufficient rest

Pet Profile Generator

It utilises machine learning to analyse data about your pet's personality, behaviours, and needs. It also generates reports describing their characteristics, such as the pet's traits, likes, and dislikes.

Pet Recommendation System

A recommendation algorithm that finds the best products, services, or activities for your pet based on their profile.

Pet Social Network and Compatibility

It uses a social networking platform to connect you with other pet owners who have pets that are compatible with yours. You can chat, share photos, and plan playdates.
